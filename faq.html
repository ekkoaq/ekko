Payments

Payments are primarily accepted through PayPal and GCash, although alternative payment methods may be discussed when available.

My standard payment structure is:

  • 60% Down Payment before work begins
  • Remaining 40% upon project completion

Clients may also choose to pay 100% upfront if preferred.

All commission prices are listed in USD (United States Dollars).

Any conversion fees or exchange rate differences will depend on your payment provider.

A down payment must be received before any work begins on your commission.

This helps secure your slot in the queue and confirms your commitment to the project.

Due to the custom nature of commission work, payments are generally non-refundable once work has started.

You may request a cancellation before work has begun.

If work has already started, refunds may not be available, especially if significant progress has already been made.

Minor revisions are usually included.

However, excessive revisions or major redesign requests after approval may incur additional charges depending on the amount of work required.
